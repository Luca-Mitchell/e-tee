Materials

Our tees are unique in both manufacturing process and material. We use recycled paper from local schools and a homemade biodegradable glue formulation to create strong, composite material which maintains the biodegradable nature at the core of our golf tees. Our materials ensure a safe environment for flora and fauna to flourish, unlike toxic plastic tees and sharp bamboo tees. With almost 5 billion tees used each year, these seemingly insignificant problems quickly spiral out of control. In fact, many golf courses are in the opening stages of banning plastic tees altogether.

Fast Biodegrading

Estimates show approximately 80% of the 5 million UK golfers use plastic tees, which can remain in nature for around 450 years before completely disappearing, all whilst emitting toxic microplastics into the ground, killing vegetation. Our tees have no such drawbacks, as well as being completely carbon neutral which makes us the perfect choice for those of the climate conscious mindset. Whilst other solutions exist, we still believe ours to be the best. Our research shows that our tees can biodegrade around 3 times faster than bamboo tees.

Our Tees VS Bamboo Tees

A prominent issue surrounding bamboo, wood and plastic tees is their hardness, which over time may dull golf course maintainence equipment. In addition to this, when bamboo tees break, sharp debris remains which can be damaging to the local wildlife. This is without even mentioning the environmental impact of shipping acres of bamboo forest all the way from Jiangxi, China. The paper tees we sell can be forgotten about with peace of mind; no more searching in the forest for a stray pink plastic piece of litter, our tees pose no threat at all. Within a few months to a year, nothing is left, except for the beautiful course you know and love.

ABOUT US

Elevate your game with our eco-friendly tees, crafted from locally sourced recycled paper. Championing sustainability, our tees reinforce our commitment to a cleaner environment, both on and off the course. Manufactured entirely in the UK, our tees outpace the competition by biodegrading up to 3x faster, thanks to our distinctive recycled paper composite material. Redefining the stereotype around paper products, our tees are not only strong and durable but also reusable. With an innovative biodegradable dextrin binder, they match the strength of traditional bamboo and wood - no need to worry about bent or broken tees.
